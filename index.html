About Bill's Fine Woodworking

With over 20 years of experience in fine woodworking, I've developed a reputation for quality craftsmanship and attention to detail. Each project is approached with a commitment to excellence.

I specialize in creating custom furniture pieces that blend traditional techniques with contemporary design. Whether it's a single accent piece or a complete furniture collection, every project receives the same level of dedication.

My workshop is equipped with traditional hand tools and modern machinery, allowing me to tackle projects of any complexity. I work exclusively with high-quality hardwoods and premium finishes.
