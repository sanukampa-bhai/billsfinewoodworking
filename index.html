About Bill Robell

Bill Robell has been building things his entire life. With roots in a San Diego construction family and over four decades of hands-on experience, he brings a depth of knowledge that spans every phase of the building process — from framing to the finest finish work.

His specialties include custom cabinetry, kitchen and bathroom builds, bookshelves, furniture, and architectural millwork, all crafted from premium hardwoods. Bill is also an accomplished boatbuilder, bringing the same precision and eye for detail that marine craft demand to every project he takes on.

Whether it's a full kitchen remodel or a single custom piece of furniture, Bill approaches each job with the care and craftsmanship that only a lifetime in the trade can produce.
